Tags and Elements in HTML

Tag: A tag is a piece of code in HTML that is used to define elements on a webpage. Tags are enclosed within angle brackets <>. For example, <p> is the opening paragraph tag, and </p> is the closing paragraph tag.

Element: An element refers to the combination of a tag, its attributes (if any), and the content between the opening and closing tags.

Example:
<p>This is a paragraph element.</p>
In this case:

<p> is the opening tag.
This is a paragraph element. is the content.
</p> is the closing tag. Together, these form an HTML element.
