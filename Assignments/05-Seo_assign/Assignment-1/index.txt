What is SEO and the Importance of SEO
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) refers to the process of improving a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) to attract more organic (non-paid) traffic. SEO involves optimizing various aspects of a website, including its content, structure, and technical performance, to make it more appealing to search engines like Google.

Importance of SEO:

Increases Visibility and Traffic: A higher ranking in search results leads to increased website traffic.
Builds Credibility and Trust: Users tend to trust websites that rank highly in search engines.
Improves User Experience: SEO includes optimizing content and website design, leading to a better user experience.
Cost-Effective Marketing: SEO brings organic traffic, reducing the need for paid advertising.
Boosts Brand Awareness: Appearing on the first page of search results increases brand recognition.
